Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2025

SilverHealthGuide ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you visit our website at silverhealthguide.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as your email address if you sign up for our newsletter. We also collect information automatically through cookies and analytics tools, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on our site.

How We Use Your Information

  • To send you our newsletter (only if you subscribed)
  • To analyze site traffic and improve our content
  • To serve relevant advertisements through our ad network partners
  • To comply with legal obligations

Advertising

We participate in advertising programs including Google AdSense, Monetag, and Adsterra. These third-party vendors use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices or optout.networkadvertising.org.

Cookies

We use cookies to analyze traffic (Google Analytics) and to serve advertisements. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects information anonymously. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third-Party Links

Our site may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Data Retention

We retain email addresses of newsletter subscribers until you unsubscribe. Analytics data is retained for 26 months per Google Analytics default settings.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@silverhealthguide.com.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will post the updated date at the top of this page.

Contact Us
